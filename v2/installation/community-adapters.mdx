title: Community Adapters
---

In addition to the officially supported [Laravel](https://laravel.com/) adapter, there are also
numerous community built server-side adapters available:

- [AdonisJS](https://docs.adonisjs.com/guides/inertia)
- [ASP.NET](https://github.com/kapi2289/InertiaCore)
- [CakePHP InertiaJS Plugin](https://github.com/CakeDC/cakephp-inertia)
- [CakePHP](https://github.com/ishanvyas22/cakephp-inertiajs)
- [CanJS](https://github.com/cherifGsoul/inertia-can)
- [Clojure](https://github.com/prestancedesign/inertia-clojure)
- [ColdBox](https://github.com/elpete/cbInertia)
- [Django](https://pypi.org/project/inertia-django/)
- [Echo](https://github.com/kohkimakimoto/inertia-echo)
- [Flask](https://github.com/j0ack/flask-inertia)
- [Go (gonertia)](https://github.com/romsar/gonertia)
- [Go (inertia-go)](https://github.com/petaki/inertia-go)
- [Grails](https://github.com/matrei/grails-inertia-plugin)
- [Kirby CMS](https://github.com/tobimori/kirby-inertia)
- [Mako](https://github.com/inventor96/inertia-mako/)
